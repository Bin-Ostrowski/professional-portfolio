About Me

I’m obsessed with making things and more importantly making things for people to enjoy. It started when I was six making the best smoothie I could for my mother, then grew into manipulating metals into breathtaking pieces of jewelry, and most recently into developing websites for small businesses. After graduating from Northern Michigan University, I spent years honing my skills as a metalsmith while building my own business Roski Jewelry. When I started selling pieces online, the vast opportunities in developing websites captured my interest and I dove right in.

When I’m not creating, I’m out burning off steam mountain biking, grilling fish, or swimming laps in whatever water I can find.
